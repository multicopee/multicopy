MultiCopy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. Our Privacy Commitment

MultiCopy was built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. We believe your clipboard data is personal and should stay that way. Our extension is designed to function entirely on your local machine.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

  • Clipboard Content: All text snippets you copy are stored locally in your browser's extension storage. They are never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Usage Data: We do not track which websites you visit or how you use the extension.
  • Personal Information: We do not require an account, email address, or any personal identification to use MultiCopy.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored using the chrome.storage.local API. This data stays on your physical device and is deleted if you uninstall the extension.

4. Third-Party Services

MultiCopy does not share data with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers.

5. Contact Us
